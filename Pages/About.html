National News Agency

This is a news channel site that provides news to people both the local content and international as well to read. This site is designed as to work with databases, admin and team that manages this all site.

The world is tilting toward digital news delivery; research has shown that each year the number of people using the internet as their source of news is increasing than the traditional media we know, this is pushing online newspapers to get more readers than the print media. Emphatically speaking all print media have an online version of newspapers and there are standalone news agencies publishing news in an online format only but same cannot be said about the traditional print media.

For site of this nature and the level of complexity in news delivery because of technology and the way people live based on a customisation, it is important this site goes beyond the traditional way of providing news to a level such as reader’s account for customisation, geo based push notification, reduce ads, area of interest, employee login. User and Sponsors/Customer account who want to get specific news or they want to use the advertisement service in this channel.

This site has user account service so user can get notified at whenever his important/favourite news is arrived. User can modify or add multiple favourite/important category/type of the news. User can save, like/unlike and comment about the news. The user also has a option to share link of news with anyone.

This site will also available in two languages which are ‘English’ and ‘ हिन्दी ’. The user can switch between two languages using the single button. If user modify the language the content of site is modified, site will display the content what is available in the selected language.

This site also provides Admin login and dashboard. The admin has main control over all the news, admin has control over employee's department who are working for this news system, and admin can collect information about website, employee and users.

The site provides employee login and dashboard, they can take different role in this organization, they can work in IT department, Marketing department, Reporter department etc. And they can submit their work in website.
